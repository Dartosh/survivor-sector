farm

the lands of the sector had always been the most fertile in the region before the catastrophe. It is good that nothing has changed after it, and with a professional approach and good seeds, you can harvest an excellent harvest

workshop

a place where the most intelligent characters of the sector create various parts needed to improve the camp, as well as equipment for other characters

watchtower

the watchtower is the main building in the camp. It is also the highest, so that the sentry can notice the approach of mobs in a timely manner and start preparing for defense

sawmill

a building designed for the production of building materials. Harvesting building materials is not an easy job, so strong, dexterous guys are needed here

house

a building intended for the rest of the characters, as well as for the treatment of the wounded in battle. In harsh conditions, there are not enough beds for all, so the heavily injured are placed in beds so that they recover faster
